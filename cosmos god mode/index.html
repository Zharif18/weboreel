✦ HOW TO CREATE ✦
CREATING CELESTIAL BODIES
⭐
Birth Star (Press 1) — Hold mouse button to charge power, then release to create a new star. The longer you hold, the bigger the star.
🪐
Forge Planet (Press 2) — Click near an existing star to create a planet orbiting it.
🌱
Spark Life (Press 3) — Click on a planet to ignite life. Watch civilizations emerge!
COSMIC PHENOMENA
🕳️
Black Hole (Press 4) — Hold and release to create a gravity-devouring black hole.
☄️
Comet (Press 5) — Click and drag to launch a comet across the cosmos.
🌀
Wormhole (Press 6) — Click twice to create a portal between two points in space.
🌌
Nebula (Press 7) — Click and drag to paint beautiful nebulae.
DESTRUCTION & DISCOVERY
💫
Supernova (Press 8) — Click on a star to trigger a spectacular explosion.
💥
Destroy (Press 9) — Click on any object to annihilate it completely.
🔭
Inspect (Press 0) — Click on any celestial body to view its details.
TIME CONTROL
⏸
Bottom Right Controls — Pause, play at normal speed, or accelerate time (1×, 3×, 10×).
